The Open Source Musician Podcast is back!

Updated: Wed, 2015-11-25 13:45
Category: 
general

Spencer Jackson has just announced the return of The Open Source Musician Podcast. The Open Source Musician Podcast has also moved to archive.org, where you can find the whole catalog of OSM podcast episodes.

Spencer returns with episode 71, where he talks to Filipe Coelho, AKA falkTX, founder and maintainer of KXStudio about his work on the MOD project.

For full details, check out Spencer's post at Linux Audio Announcements.
