LMP Asks Interview

For our second interview of the year, we talked to Damien Zammit, developer of zam-plugins, contributor to the coreboot project, Calf studio plugins and Linux drivers for various audio interfaces. Thanks to Damien for the interview!

LMP content wish list progress

Due to progress made on tutorials, we've recently been able to tick off a few tasks from the content wish list. We would like to now ask the LMP community for more suggestions of what content you would like to see on the website. If you have any suggestions, feel free to add them in the comments section of the content wish list.

Also, if anyone is interested in contributing any content, the wish list is a good place to look first. Maybe you have knowledge of a certain piece of software on the list and would like to write and contribute some work to the LMP community. If anyone is willing to tackle any of the tasks in the wish list, please contact us.
